Portugal is famous for its seafood and pastries.

Try "Bacalhau" (codfish) and "Pastel de Nata" !

Bacalhau is one of Portugals most famous traditional dishes — and it’s made from dried and salted codfish . The main ingredient is codfish (a type of white fish) that has been preserved with salt and dried.
